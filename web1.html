Why Renewable energy Mining?


"According to estimates, bitcoin mining consumes 136 terawatt-hours (TWh) every year. The mining industry may be able to influence energy producers to install more renewable sources as it decarbonizes. Mining with renewable energy is the route to go. Using renewable electricity makes a lot of economic sense for many miners. Overall, renewable energy is less expensive than fossil fuels, allowing miners to keep their profitability."
Bitcoin mining is a necessary component of the economy. It will be here for the duration of this existence, which appears to be infinite at this moment. The most ethical strategy to reduce mining power use is to start from within. This transition comprises a shift to renewable energy and other environmentally friendly options.
